Space Invaders

Instructions

Keyboard Control

Press:

- 'A' & 'D' keys to move left/right

- 'Space' key to shoot

- 'M' key to launch missiles

- 'B' key to launch nuke

- 'V' key to switch aircraft

Game Play

Info:

- Players lives = 3

- Nukes available = 2

- Shoot the UFOs to score points

- Avoid UFOs from reaching the ground
